About

I am a Computer Science major who is enthusiastic about the current developments of technology. Because game and app development is very interdisciplinary, I often undertake personal projects in these areas to exercise my talents in art and design. While I love having my own creative freedom, team projects are not foreign to me. I love seeing my own ideas improve with other's input and enjoy trying to creatively twist ideas suggested to me as well.
